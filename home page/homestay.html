Woodbrook Homestay

A homestay is a type of lodging where guests live with a local family. It generally means that You Have Your Own Room and that You Can Be More Private or Alone, Depending on your Family or Your Preferences. The service is above and beyond what you would receive in a hotel because the majority of the hosting families are so hospitable.The homestay is a very special place and exploring a strange culture.
